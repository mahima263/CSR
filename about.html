Corporate Social Responsibility at IIT Kanpur

A Commitment to Inclusive Progress

At the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, we hold a steadfast belief that institutions of higher learning have a responsibility that goes beyond academic excellence and scientific advancement. Our ethos is rooted in the principle of “Together We Grow”, which underpins every initiative we undertake to create a more equitable, sustainable, and inclusive society.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is not simply a statutory requirement in our view—it is a shared commitment to national development, where academia and industry collaborate to address the most pressing challenges facing our communities.

Inclusive Growth

Knowledge matched with duty — ensuring those excluded from its benefits are empowered.

Diverse Domains

Programs in education, healthcare, clean energy, digital inclusion, sustainability, and more.

Collaborative Impact

Corporates, academia, and communities co-designing scalable, research-led solutions.

“At IIT Kanpur, CSR is not a checkbox — it is a pledge we renew every day, through action, reflection, and impact.”
