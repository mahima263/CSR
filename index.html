Munh Parikshak

Empowering Everyone with the Power of Timely Diagnosis.

The Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK) has developed and successfully transferred a pioneering healthcare innovation, Munh Parikshak â€” a portable device designed for the early detection of oral cancer. Developed by Prof. Jayant Kumar Singh and his team from the Department of Chemical Engineering, the device utilizes specialized lighting and an in-built camera to examine the oral cavity and instantly analyze mouth images using advanced algorithms. Based on the analysis, the condition is categorized as normal, pre-cancerous, or cancerous, with results displayed through a user-friendly smartphone application.

These results are securely stored on cloud servers for remote consultations and long-term tracking. The technology has been transferred to Scangenie Scientific Pvt. Ltd. for large-scale deployment. As oral cancer remains one of Indiaâ€™s leading health concerns, especially in underserved areas, Munh Parikshak offers a low-cost, scalable solution that exemplifies IIT Kanpurâ€™s focus on socially relevant innovation.

Digital Health Stack

Transforming Healthcare Delivery through Digital Innovation

Launched in 2024 by IIT Kanpur with support from the ICICI Foundation, the Digital Health Stack (DHS) project bridges critical healthcare gaps in Uttar Pradesh through telemedicine, secure data integration, and AI-led solutions. By enhancing digital platforms at primary and secondary care levels, DHS is reducing the burden on tertiary hospitals and expanding access for rural populations.

The project has influenced state policy, piloted a scalable telemedicine system, and developed a secure Health Information Exchange (HIE) aligned with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission. DHS even built a full referral and ambulance system in just two weeks for the 2025 Maha Kumbh, benefitting thousands. With strong collaborations with NHA and DHR, it stands as a replicable model for Indiaâ€™s digital health infrastructure.

CSR DOMAINS

Driving Impact Across Key Social Domains

At IIT Kanpur, our CSR initiatives focus on creating sustainable impact in Healthcare, Environment, Education, Research, Community Welfare, and Infrastructure. Through innovation, collaboration, and community engagement, we transform ideas into actionable programs that benefit society and align with national development goals.

01
Healthcare

IIT Kanpur supports community health through affordable medical technologies, preventive health awareness, and accessible healthcare delivery models. Initiatives include non-invasive portable devices, diagnostic tools, and AI-driven health monitoring for underserved regions. Aligned with SDG 3 and Ayushman Bharat, these efforts strengthen resilient public health infrastructure.

02
Environment & Sustainability

IIT Kanpur promotes water conservation, renewable energy, waste management, and climate resilience. Technologies for greywater reuse, sustainable agriculture, and eco-restoration address critical ecological challenges. Aligned with SDG 6, SDG 13, and national missions like Jal Shakti Abhiyan, IITK contributes to long-term environmental stewardship.

03
Education, Skill & Development

Inclusive learning through digital education, vocational training, and teacher empowerment initiatives. Supporting rural education via interactive content and customized pedagogy, and skill development in trades like tailoring, electronics, and computer literacy. Aligned with SDG 4 and Skill India Mission, empowering lifelong learning and self-reliance.

04
Research & Innovation

IIT Kanpur drives socially relevant research through problem-oriented innovation. CSR-enabled labs and incubators support early-stage ideas addressing grassroots challenges. Technology transfer and capacity building foster community-centric solutions aligned with SDG 9 and the Atal Innovation Mission.

05
Community & Welfare

Focused on improving life in rural and peri-urban areas through self-help groups, farmer clusters, and artisan support. Initiatives promote income generation, nutrition, sanitation, and social equity, aligned with SDG 1, SDG 5, and Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana.

06
Infrastructure

Supporting rural and semi-urban infrastructure with clean water systems, renewable energy setups, smart classrooms, and lab upgrades. Projects are need-assessed and community-driven, aligned with SDG 11, PM Awas Yojana, and Swachh Bharat Mission.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Class Legacy Fund?

A Class Legacy Fund is a fund created by a specific batch or alumni group to support various initiatives at IIT Kanpur. It allows graduates from a particular year or batch to come together and contribute towards the institute's development.

How will my contribution be used?

Your contribution will be used to support various initiatives, such as scholarships, campus infrastructure, faculty development, and more. The purpose for which the funds will be utilized will be decided unanimously by the batch.

Is there a limit on tax exemption if I contribute to IITK?

There is no limit on the amount for tax exemption if one contributes to IITK. There is 100% tax exemption.

What is a Recurring Contribution?

A recurring contribution authorises a specific amount of money to be automatically deducted from your bank account, credit card, or any other payment methods at predetermined intervals.

Can I cancel a recurring contribution?

Yes, one can cancel the recurring contribution at any point of time. Please write to us with the details of your contribution at dora_desk@iitk.ac.in.

Can I set up recurring contributions or pledge a long-term commitment to IIT Kanpur?

Yes, one can set up recurring contributions or pledge a long-term commitment to IIT Kanpur. This allows one to provide continuous support to the institute projects and initiatives over a longer duration of time.

Can I make a one-time contribution, or are recurring contributions preferred?

Both one-time contributions and recurring contributions are welcome. As of now, the recurring contributions are only for Indian Citizens or Alumni living in India.

How can I get in touch with the fundraising or development office at IITK?

Please feel free to write to us at dora_desk@iitk.ac.in or vp_development@iitk.ac.in in case of any questions or concerns.

Can I pledge in one go and donate it over a period of time?

One can choose to contribute in one go or over a period of time depending on your convenience. You can start pledging now at Pledge Form.

Can I get updates on the impact of my contribution?

Yes, the institute provides regular updates and reports on how contributions are used and their impact on the institution and its students.

Does my employer offer a matching gift program for contributions?

In the U.S, some employers have matching gift programs where they match or multiply their employees' charitable contributions. Check with your employer to see if such a program is available, which can significantly increase the impact of one's contribution. Please look for 'IITK Foundation Inc' or Indian Institute of Kanpur Foundation Inc in your company's list of approved organisations.

Can I make corporate contributions or explore partnerships with IIT Kanpur?

Yes, corporations can support IIT Kanpur through its CSR as well as sponsored research programs. Many corporations have already contributed to IIT Kanpur through corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives that support educational institutions. Please write to us at dora_desk@iitk.ac.in for more information.

How can I make a contribution in memory of someone?

Many people choose to give to IITK in memory-or in honour of a family member, classmate, faculty member, or friend. Please drop us a mail at dora_desk@iitk.ac.in and we will be glad to share and explore the possibilities.

Will I receive a receipt of my contributions?

Yes, IITK issues a receipt for all its contributions. This receipt is an important document for tax purposes and can be used to claim any eligible tax benefits.

Can I make an anonymous contribution?

Yes, anonymous contributions are allowed at IITK. Please click on the check box of anonymous contributions while making the contribution.
